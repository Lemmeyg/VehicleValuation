# About Sarah Johnson Auto Appraisal

Sarah Johnson has been providing certified auto appraisals in the Chicago area for over 15 years. With extensive expertise in total loss claims and diminished value assessments, Sarah specializes in helping accident victims navigate complex insurance claims and secure the maximum settlements they deserve.

## Services Offered

- **Independent Vehicle Appraisals**: Unbiased third-party valuations for insurance claims
- **Total Loss Claims**: Expert assistance in disputing low settlement offers from insurance companies
- **Pre-Accident Value Assessment**: Accurate market valuations to establish your vehicle's true worth
- **Diminished Value Claims**: Calculate post-accident value loss for properly repaired vehicles
