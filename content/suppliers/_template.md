# About Smith Auto Appraisal Services

John Smith has been providing independent vehicle appraisals in the Chicago area since 2009. With over 15 years of experience in the automotive industry, John specializes in helping vehicle owners who have been in accidents get fair compensation from insurance companies.

## Services Offered

- **Independent Vehicle Appraisals**: Unbiased third-party valuations for insurance claims
- **Total Loss Claim Support**: Expert assistance in disputing low settlement offers
- **Diminished Value Assessments**: Calculate post-accident value loss for your vehicle
- **Pre-purchase Inspections**: Comprehensive vehicle evaluations before you buy
